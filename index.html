Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering

Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering (SJCE), established under the umbrella of JSS Science and Technology University (JSSSTU), stands as a premier institution for technical education. Recognized globally for its rigorous academic infrastructure, the university fosters an environment optimized for engineering excellence, system-level innovation, and foundational computing sciences.
